# Privacy

ClientPad is open-source business infrastructure. Self-hosted deployments are controlled by the developer or business operating them.

For hosted ClientPad Cloud, we process only the information needed to operate the service:

- operator account details such as name and email;
- workspace and project metadata;
- API keys and usage records;
- lead, client, and workflow data submitted by the customer;
- integration diagnostics needed for support, reliability, and abuse prevention.

We do not sell customer data. We use service providers such as hosting, database, email, analytics, and payment providers to operate ClientPad Cloud.

Customers can contact us to request account or workspace deletion, subject to security, fraud-prevention, legal, and accounting retention requirements.
