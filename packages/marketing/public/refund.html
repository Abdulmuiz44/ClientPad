Refund Policy

ClientPad sells optional hosted Cloud access and optional implementation support. The open-source software remains free under the repository license.

Hosted Cloud subscriptions

Customers can cancel future renewals at any time. If a hosted Cloud subscription does not work as described and we cannot resolve the issue, customers can request a refund within 14 days of the initial purchase.

Refunds are not normally provided for completed billing periods after the service has been actively used, except where required by law or where ClientPad materially failed to provide the paid hosted service.

Custom implementation support

Custom setup, deployment, or integration work is quoted separately. Refund eligibility depends on whether work has started, the agreed scope, and the deliverables already completed.

Contact

Refund requests can be sent through the contact page with the account email, workspace name, order reference, and reason for the request.
